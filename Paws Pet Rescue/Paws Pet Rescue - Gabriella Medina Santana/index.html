Welcome to a Place of Hope & Happy Tails!

Paws Pet Rescue is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need. We provide a safe and loving environment for cats and dogs while they wait for their forever families. Our mission is driven by compassion and the belief that every animal deserves a second chance.

Our Mission

To rescue animals from high-risk situations, provide necessary medical care and socialization, and find loving, permanent homes through careful adoption screening. We also strive to educate the community on responsible pet ownership.
