Our Story: Compassion in Action

Paws Pet Rescue was founded in 2018 by a small group of dedicated animal lovers who saw a need for a compassionate, community-focused rescue in the area. What started as a foster-based network quickly grew, thanks to the incredible support of volunteers and donors.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every animal deserves a chance at a happy, healthy life in a loving home. We are committed to:

  • Providing high-quality medical care and rehabilitation.
  • Ensuring thorough adoption screening to make lasting matches.
  • Promoting spaying/neutering to reduce pet overpopulation.
  • Treating every animal and person with respect and kindness.
  • Operating with transparency and fiscal responsibility.
